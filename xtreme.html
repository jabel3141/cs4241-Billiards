X-Treme Cue Sports

X-Treme Cue Sports is a game designed by members of the Cue Sports Club (at WPI) in order to add a new level of excitement while playing pool with your friends.

How to Play:

Tap the button before every shot to generate a card. (before the break as well)

The player must perform the action dictated by the card.

All standard rules apply unless stated otherwise by a card.

Honor Rule: Players must always attempt the shot as stated. Failure to do so results in Ball in Hand for the opponent.

If it is ever Ball in Hand, the player must place the Cue ball down on the table before drawing a card for their turn.

Note: Please respect the equipment despite all of these additional rules.
